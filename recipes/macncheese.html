Mac-n-Cheese!!!

This is a completely unpretentious, down-home macaroni and cheese recipe just like my grandma and mom always made. A simple white sauce where you add cheese and a few other flavors with macaroni and a potato chip crust.

his recipe makes a lot, I like to make two pans so I can take one to a pot luck and have the other one to keep in the fridge for later. It's an easy recipe to adjust down if you only want to make one pan. If you aren't cooking it right away, just keep the topping separate until you are ready to put it in the oven.

Ingredients

  • Cup butter
  • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Powders
  • Cooking spray
  • Macaroni
  • American cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes; drain.
  3. ...Bake in preheated oven until the crust is golden brown and the sauce is bubbling, 35 to 45 minutes.
